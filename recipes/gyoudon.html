Gyoudon recipe

Description

Gyudon is a Japanese beef bowl consisting of thinly sliced fatty beef and onions in a lightly sweet mixture of mirin and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Onion
  • Sauce
  • Garnish

Steps

  1. Cut the onion, green onions, and beef.
  2. Put the sauce ingredients, sliced onions, and beef in a large frying pan and start cooking.
  3. Serve the simmered beef and onion over steamed rice in large bowls.
